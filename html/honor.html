THE PROJECT

Our goal is to model new features to improve Airbnb application's user experience.

MY ROLE

My role in this project is UI + UX design.

RESEARCH

Who the primary users of the interface are, what are the goals of these users?

We found that people contact with Airbnb they typically have a strong, emotional sense of purpose for the room they want to rent or live. The primary user are travelers who prefer to live in places with full amenities and a feeling like home and who looking to rent their homes with people. Their goals are find a flexcible space to live and monetize their extra space.

PERSONA

INSIGHT

Some users choose Airbnb is because they are usually have more than two people traveling. Thus, comparing to hotels, Airbnb is more flexible.

IDEA

We designed "Joint Account" feature in order to make multiple users who are going to live in the room together during their trip much easier and more effucent. Everyone is able to talk to the host and receiev everything from the room they choose.
